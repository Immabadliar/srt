Careers with HDA SRT

Explore the different career opportunities available with the HDA Special Response Team.

Operative (E-2 - E-6 / O2 - O6)

Perform high-risk missions and provide support to the team in critical operations.

Command Sergeant Major (E-9)

Provide guidance and leadership to enlisted personnel in the SRT.

Sergeant Major (E-9)

Ensure the readiness and effectiveness of the enlisted force.

Medic

Provide medical support during high-risk operations and emergencies.

Sniper

Perform specialized precision shooting and support long-range operations.

Team Leader (Sergeant / Lieutenant)

Lead and guide teams in critical missions, ensuring operational success.

Academy Instructor (Staff Sergeant / Major)

Train and mentor new recruits to prepare them for operations in the field.
